A croissant is a French pastry, traditionally crescent-shaped and made from a laminated yeast dough, resulting in a buttery, flaky, and airy texture.

A good croissant should be golden brown, crispy, and flaky on the outside, with a soft and airy interior.

Croissants are commonly enjoyed for breakfast or as a snack, often paired with coffee or tea.
